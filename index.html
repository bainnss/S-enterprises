about us

S.Enterprises had been formed in the year 2013 under the Leadership of
Mr. Mohammed Kamaluddin Shershawadi

We have constructed many landmark buildings. Come to us for a worry-free, high quality experience for everything relating to construction. We have infinite practical experience of Civil Construction in Residential Commercial, Factory Buildings, School Buildings & Malls. The firm has carried out Building Projects at Thane, Mulund, Mumbai, Navi Mumbai and Puna. & still carrying out. The firm has a complete ISI Standardized Laboratory set-up, so that quality checks can be done regularly. The firm has adequate strength along with Technical Engineer, Supervisors, Skilled & un-skilled labor of all categories.
